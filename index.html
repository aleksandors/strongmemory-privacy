Privacy Policy for StrongMemory

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Introduction

StrongMemory ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information.

Information We Collect

StrongMemory does not collect or store any personal information. All training data (statistics, records, settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information to show relevant ads, including device identifiers and usage data. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to remind you about Long Training sessions. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and no data is sent to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
